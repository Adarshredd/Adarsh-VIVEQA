# Day 04 Notes: Advanced Flip-Flops

Today's topics were the JK Flip-Flop and flip-flop conversions. A JK Flip-Flop provides Hold, Set, Reset, and Toggle states, overcoming the invalid state of the SR Flip-Flop. We also demonstrated how to build a D Flip-Flop using a JK Flip-Flop by simply tying J to D and K to NOT D.
